About

Head of Innovation at NEVERHACK, specialized in applying artificial intelligence to cybersecurity, with a particular focus on reinforcement learning, intrusion detection systems, and genetic programming.

Research Areas

  • Artificial Intelligence for Cybersecurity
  • Reinforcement Learning and Tangled Program Graphs (TPG)
  • Intrusion Detection Systems
  • Machine Learning for Binary Classification
  • Supply Chain Security
